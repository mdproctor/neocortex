---
title: Lease Termination Provisions
domain: legal
tags: lease, termination, notice
---

Either party may terminate this Lease prior to the expiration of the Term upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice to the other party. Notice shall be deemed delivered when sent by certified mail, return receipt requested, to the address specified in Section 22 (Notices), or when personally delivered with written acknowledgment of receipt. Email or facsimile transmission shall not constitute valid notice for purposes of early termination under this Section.

Tenant electing early termination shall pay a termination fee equal to three (3) months' Base Rent as of the effective termination date. The termination fee shall be paid concurrently with delivery of the termination notice and is non-refundable. Failure to pay the termination fee within the notice period shall render the termination notice void and of no effect.

Upon early termination, Tenant shall surrender the Premises in the condition required by Section 18 (Surrender of Premises). Landlord shall conduct a final inspection within seven (7) business days of the termination date. Any damages beyond ordinary wear and tear shall be documented in writing with photographic evidence. The Security Deposit shall be applied first to unpaid Rent, then to repair costs for damages, then to cleaning costs if required, with any remaining balance returned to Tenant within thirty (30) days.

Notwithstanding the foregoing, early termination shall not release Tenant from any obligations that accrued prior to the termination date.
