Contractor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to Company all right, title, and interest in and to all Work Product created by Contractor in connection with Services performed under this Agreement. Work Product includes all inventions, discoveries, improvements, designs, software, documentation, and other materials, whether or not patentable or copyrightable, and whether or not reduced to practice. The assignment under this Section includes all intellectual property rights worldwide, including copyrights, patents, trade secrets, moral rights, and all rights to register, renew, and enforce such rights.

Contractor represents and warrants that: (a) Contractor is the sole author and owner of all Work Product delivered under this Agreement; (b) the Work Product does not infringe any third party intellectual property rights; (c) Contractor has not granted and will not grant any rights in the Work Product to any third party; and (d) Contractor has obtained all necessary consents, releases, and permissions for use of any third-party materials incorporated into the Work Product.

Contractor shall execute any documents and perform any acts reasonably requested by Company to confirm Company's ownership of Work Product, including execution of patent applications, copyright registrations, and assignments. If Contractor fails to execute such documents within ten (10) days of request, Contractor hereby appoints Company as attorney-in-fact to execute such documents on Contractor's behalf.

Contractor retains ownership of pre-existing materials incorporated into Work Product, provided that Contractor grants Company a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free license to use such materials.
